Frequently Asked Questions

What is ThejasLink?

ThejasLink is a centralized healthcare platform that allows patients to securely access their medical records, prescriptions, and test results from anywhere. It also provides a dashboard for doctors to manage appointments, patients, and records efficiently.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is highly secure. We use advanced encryption protocols and adhere to strict privacy standards to ensure your medical information is protected at all times.

Can I access my records offline?

Currently, ThejasLink requires an internet connection to access and sync your records. We are working on an offline access feature for future updates.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team by clicking the "Contact Support" link in the dashboard or by calling the number provided at the bottom of the page. We are available 24/7 to assist you.
